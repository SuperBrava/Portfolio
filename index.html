About.Me

The development of web technologies over the last 20 years have been truly exciting. It has allowed the entire world to become more connected than ever before in human history. From a young age I had a natural interest in coding/markup languages which even led me to design various flash games using ActionScript 2.0 (which will be forever lost in the archives of newgrounds). I am always eager to learn new web languages, frameworks, libraries and software packages through excellent training at Treehouse to improve my own abilities in modern web design.

When I'm not behind a computer screen I like to keep active by cross-country mountain biking. This has allowed me to explore amazing places both at home and abroad; including charity rides, races and even biking through the Alps region of Les Arcs. I'm also a big fan of music and have been lucky enough to have seen the likes of bands such as AC/DC.

I now aim to bring my enthusiasm, creativity and attitude into a new venture where I can make a contribution to designing exciting new projects, meeting new people and learning new skills along the way.
